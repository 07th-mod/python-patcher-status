Our Hou Plus mod was initially released 2024/02/07, and at this stage shouldn't contain any major bugs.

So feel free to try it out and give us feedback.

Please note that Original/Ryukishi Sprites and Backgrounds are not yet available.

Please provide feedback and bug reports on the Hou Plus Github Issues Page, or on our Discord in the #higu_support channel


For details and screenshots on what the mod contains, see the Intro page on our Wiki
This mod contains:
  • Full voiceover
  • In-game Background Options
    • PS3 backgrounds and CGs in Full HD
    • NOT YET AVAILABLE: Original Japanese release Backgrounds
  • In-game Sprite Options
    • PS3 sprites with lipsync in 1440p
    • Updated MangaGamer sprites in 1440p
    • NOT YET AVAILABLE: Original Japanese release sprites
  • Upgraded user interface
  • Opening videos
  • In-game switchable Background Music and Sound Effects (BGM/SE):
    • We highly recommend checking out our BGM/SE FAQ
    • New BGM - The BGM/SE introduced in the April 2019 update and/or Hou+ (vs. the non-plus version)
    • Original BGM - The BGM/SE in the Hou (Non-Plus) release. See the BGM/SE FAQ for more details
